Mental Health Self-Check

This brief assessment can help you reflect on your mental health and determine if you might benefit from professional support. This is not a diagnostic tool.

Over the past two weeks, how often have you felt down, depressed, or hopeless?

How often have you felt nervous, anxious, or on edge?

How well have you been able to cope with daily stress?

How satisfied are you with your sleep quality?

How connected do you feel to friends, family, or community?

Your Results

Remember: This assessment is for educational purposes only and does not replace professional medical advice. If you're experiencing thoughts of self-harm or suicide, please reach out for help immediately.
